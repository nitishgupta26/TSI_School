INFRASTRUCTURE

CLASSROOMS

Where creativity meets excellence. And excellence meets ecstasy.

In keeping with global educational trends, TSI group of Schools has ergonomically designed buildings where students can bring out the best in them. With well-ventilated and cheerfully colored classrooms, the school also has playgrounds and attached indoor sporting facility. With its own auditorium, swimming pool, basketball court, tennis court, library etc., the young learners will be able to face the competitive world with courage and fortitude, attuning them to the magic of our great, big, beautiful world.

At TSI Group of school, it is well understood that an intellectually challenging environment is essential, in the process of learning and achievement. Therefore it has been ensured that classrooms are not only spacious but are also fully air conditioned and each class has an interactive smart class set-up powered with digital learning resources.
