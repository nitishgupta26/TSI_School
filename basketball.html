INFRASTRUCTURE

BASKETBALL COURT

Where creativity meets excellence. And excellence meets ecstasy.

In keeping with global educational trends, TSI Group Of School has ergonomically designed buildings where students can bring out the best in them. With well-ventilated and cheerfully colored classrooms, the school also has playgrounds and attached indoor sporting facility. With its own auditorium, swimming pool, basketball court, tennis court, library etc., the young learners will be able to face the competitive world with courage and fortitude, attuning them to the magic of our great, big, beautiful world.

Basketball seems to especially teach the students about the necessary fitness levels needed to stay healthy in life. It also helps athletes to learn other social skills necessary in the real world where collaboration, preparation and intellect are the key. The basketball court of school is one of its kind. Its synthetic flooring makes it all the more captivating. The indoor basketball court is air conditioned and is spacious enough to accomodatea large audience.
